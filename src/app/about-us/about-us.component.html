Our Mission

Rare disease clinical research has a magnitude of obstacles - medical, scientific, and operational- but with our passion, expertise, and compassion, we can lessen the burden of these challenges. Our multidisciplinary team is well equipped with years of clinical research experience to conduct clinical trials for patients with rare diseases. With over 10 years of clinical research experience, our facilities capabilities, and our partnerships, we are able to provide access to investigational treatments to patients with rare conditions, who otherwise would not have the opportunity to participate in cutting-edge investigational therapies. There are nearly 7,000 rare diseases and less than 400 treatments approved by the FDA, conveying the excessive lack of approved treatments for this population. With the advent of molecular understanding and discovery, we now have a unique opportunity to identify potential treatments through clinical research for rare conditions that desperately need therapies. It is our hope, with our dedication to clinical research in rare diseases, to lessen the void that exists today in clinical research participation opportunities.
