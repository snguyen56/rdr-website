Our Team

RDR is closely monitoring the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic. As the current situation continues to change, our utmost priority is the health and safety of RDR staff, patients, and their families.


RDR will incorporate mandatory measures such as wearing masks, social distancing where possible, temperature checks, and frequent cleaning and sanitation to ensure patient safety and minimize exposure. We have masks available, as well as hand sanitizer throughout our facilities to help facilitate minimizing the spread of the virus.

Staff

We are a group of physicians and coordinators dedicated to clinical research. Our team consists of exemplary staff with specialties relating to neurology, muscular dystrophy, pediatric medicine, emergency care, and many other specialties. Our approach is to focus on patient care in administering the trial investigational products to produce the clearest and most concise data possible. With our patients, our facilities, and our operating procedures, the safety and privacy of our patients and employees are our top priority.


We have a staff we are extremely proud of. A group of professionals who are the best at what they do and who enjoy their work. Together, our doctors and staff offer a superior level of treatment and service. We are committed to providing you with the best in rare disease research.
